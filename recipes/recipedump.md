https://cooking.nytimes.com/recipes/1015820-rosemary-shortbread

200g ww flour
80g ww pastry
120g sugar
12g finely chopped fresh rosemary
8g kosher salt
6g lemon zest
230g unsalted cold butter, cut into 1-inch chunks
25g maple syrup
In a medium bowl mix together flours, sugar, rosemary, salt, zest. 

Add in maple syrup and stir together.

Add cubed butter

Mix, but not overly so, will have loose flour 
press firmly into 8 by 8 parchment lined pan (have parchment high for easy removal and flatten

Chill 20 min while preheating oven to 325

Bake 30 minutes, pale but not golden and nearly firm to the touch, 

Take out, cut into pieces

Carefully separate and sprinkle salt
Bake 30 minutes
